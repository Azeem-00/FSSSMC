Welcome to FSSMC Learning Page

Our mission is to guide members of our community on the path of knowledge and spirituality. At FSSMC, we provide structured learning for all ages—children, teens, and adults—covering the Quran, Hadith, prayer, ablutions, daily duas, and Islamic classes tailored to help you grow in understanding and practice.

Read and Understand the Quran

Our Quran learning program is designed for beginners and advanced learners alike. Students will learn proper Arabic recitation with Tajweed, memorize selected Surahs, and explore the meaning and context of the verses to deepen their understanding.

  • Start with short Surahs and common prayers
  • Recitation with Tajweed guidance
  • Translation and understanding of verses
Read The Quran

Understand Hadith

Hadith are the sayings and traditions of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ that guide daily life. Our classes help learners understand authentic Hadith collections, the context behind them, and practical applications for personal development and moral conduct.

  • Start with concise, authentic Hadith
  • Learn the meaning and application in daily life
  • Interactive discussions to encourage understanding
Learn Hadith

Prayer & Ablution

Step-by-step instructions on performing Salah and Wudu. Our experienced instructors demonstrate each action, ensuring that students understand the movements, recitations, and etiquette of prayer.

  • Correct method of ablution (Wudu)
  • Step-by-step Salah instructions
  • Learning Sunnah prayers and their benefits
Learn To Pray Salah & Perform Wudu

Daily Duas

Our Dua sessions introduce learners to important daily supplications, explaining their meanings and proper times. Students will learn prayers for morning, evening, before meals, for protection, and during challenges, helping integrate spirituality into daily life.

  • Morning and evening supplications
  • Duas before and after meals
  • Protection, guidance, and gratitude duas
Read Duas

Islamic Classes for All Ages

Our Islamic classes are designed to nurture faith, knowledge, and practice for children, teens, and adults. Each age group has structured learning paths, ensuring lessons are age-appropriate, engaging, and spiritually enriching.

Children (Ages 5-12)

Classes for young children focus on foundational Islamic knowledge, teaching them basic Quranic recitation, simple duas, manners, and stories of the Prophets. Learning is interactive, with activities, quizzes, and recitation practice to make it fun and memorable.

Teens (Ages 13-18)

Teen classes emphasize deeper understanding of Quran and Hadith, Islamic history, morals, and ethics. Teens engage in discussions, workshops, and guided reflections to strengthen their faith and develop skills to navigate daily life as practicing Muslims.

Adults

Adult classes provide comprehensive Islamic knowledge, including tafsir (Quranic interpretation), fiqh (practical jurisprudence), and advanced Hadith studies. Sessions also cover personal development, family guidance, and community responsibilities, empowering adults to lead and serve with understanding.
