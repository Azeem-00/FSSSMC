About the Mosque

FSSSMC Mosque serves as a center for Islamic worship, education, and community development.

The mosque provides daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah, Islamic classes, and spiritual guidance.

It is committed to strengthening faith and serving the Muslim community with excellence.
