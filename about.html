FSSSMC Mosque

Serving Allah Through Worship, Knowledge, and Community

A center dedicated to strengthening faith, spreading knowledge of the Quran and Sunnah, and serving the Muslim community with excellence.

Vision

To be a leading Masjid that builds strong faith based on Allah-consciousness, piety and mindfulness of Allah, serving as a spiritual shield against sins and wrongdoings.

Mission

To provide deep knowledge of the Quran and Sunnah of the Prophet (PBUH) through teaching and learning, while providing a safe and holy environment for worship and delivering sustainable services for Muslims in our community and beyond.

Core Value

To live according to the Quran and Sunnah as practised by the Prophet (PBUH) and exemplified by the Sahaba.



Spiritual Structure

Headed by Imam

Responsible for religious guidance, prayers, teachings, and spiritual leadership of the Masjid.

Administrative Structure

Headed by Amir

Responsible for organisation management, planning, policy implementation, and Masjid development.
